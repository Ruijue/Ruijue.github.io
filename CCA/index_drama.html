About NUS Chinese Drama

NUS Chinese Drama is a vibrant student organization dedicated to Chinese theatrical arts. Members participate in various productions, workshops, and cultural events throughout the academic year.

Productions

Annual theatrical performances showcasing student talent and creativity in Chinese drama.

Workshops

Regular training sessions on acting, directing, scriptwriting, and stage management.

Community

A supportive environment for students passionate about Chinese theatrical arts.
