Self Introduction

Hello, my name is Wang Ruijue. I am a current undergraduate at the National University of Singapore pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Data Science and Analytics, with a second major in Economics and a minor in Computer Science.

In addition to my education, I have had the opportunity to gain practical experience as a strategy research intern at Beavo Investment, where I cleaned raw data, designed and implemented models, and improved code efficiency by parallel programs. In terms of technical skills, I have a strong foundation in Python, R, and SQL, and had experience in utilizing them in my school projects. Also, I'm a quick learner, team player and enjoy working with new technologies and tools.

Outside of my academic and professional experiences, I am an active member of several co-curricular activities, such as the NUS Chinese Drama Club, Viva Latinus Club, Math and Data Science Society, and have participated in various volunteer experiences.

Besides, I have many other interests like Chinese Opera and ukulele. Hope to make friends with you!
